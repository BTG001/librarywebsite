1.Lending Services.

Our library allows users to borrow information resources for a period of time dictated by a user group.Lending facilitates equitable resource information among all users..

Books,audio and video visuals are loaned out at the central desk.

Lending Regulations and Penalties

  • Only students and users that are registered are able to enjoy the lending service.
  • All library users MUST produce the identification cards before borrowing any information material.
  • Lost or mutilated information material will be replaced back at a market cost plus Ksh.200 for processing charges.
  • Information materials borrowed on long term basis and not returned on due will be charged at ksh.5 per item per day.
  • Information materials borrowed on short term basis and not returned on expected time will be charged at ksh.2 after 10am,the following day.
  • Users who causes inconvinience to others in the library will be punished accoding to the penalties on the notice board.

2.Photocopy and Printing.

The following methods are available for copying and printing in our library.

  • Self-service photocopying.
  • Photocopy service by library staff.

-Self-service photocopying.

This is only done by the registered students when the required information resource is limited.We do not allow self-printing.

-Photocopy and printing by the staff.

For printing and copying of materials in huge quantities and in different collections,please contact the library counter.
